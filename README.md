# FNP-Sales-Performance-Analysis-Dashboard

### 1. FNP Sales Dashboard:

An end-to-end Excel-based analytics project designed to evaluate the sales performance of **Ferns N Petals (FNP)**, a leading online gifting company. This project focuses on analyzing order lifecycles, occasion-based revenue peaks, and logistics efficiency to drive better business decisions.

### 2. Purpose

The purpose of this dashboard is to provide a holistic view of the sales ecosystem, helping stakeholders understand how different occasions (e.g., Diwali, Rakshabandhan, Valentine's Day) impact revenue. By tracking KPIs like average delivery duration and hourly ordering patterns, the business can optimize inventory management and refine notification strategies for peak customer activity.

### 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

* **📊 Microsoft Excel** – Primary platform for data modeling and dashboard creation.
* **📂 Power Query (ETL)** – Used for extracting data from multiple CSV sources, cleaning inconsistencies, and merging product/customer details.
* **🧠 Power Pivot & DAX** – Implementation of a **Star Schema** data model and custom measures to calculate total revenue, customer spending, and delivery time differences.
* **🎨 Data Visualization** – Strategic use of Pivot Charts, Slicers, and Timelines to create an interactive and scannable UI.

### 4. Data Source

The project utilizes a comprehensive Gifting Dataset consisting of:

* **Order Metrics:** Order ID, Quantity, Order Date, and Time.
* **Logistics Data:** Delivery Date, Delivery Time, and Shipping Location.
* **Product Data:** Product Name, Category, Price (INR), and Associated Occasion.
* **Customer Data:** Customer ID, City, Gender, and Contact Information.

### 5. Features

#### Business Problem

Gifting companies experience extreme demand volatility during holidays and special occasions. Without a centralized dashboard, it is difficult for managers to:

* Identify which specific occasions drive the highest revenue.
* Determine if high order volumes correlate with longer delivery delays.
* Pinpoint the exact hours when customers are most likely to place orders to optimize ad spend.

#### Goal of the Dashboard

* **Revenue Attribution:** Identify which product categories (e.g., Sweets, Flowers, Soft Toys) perform best during specific festivals.
* **Efficiency Tracking:** Monitor the average time taken from order placement to final delivery.
* **Behavioral Analysis:** Analyze "Peak Ordering Hours" to align marketing notifications with customer browsing habits.

#### Walkthrough of Key Visuals

* **KPI Scorecard:** Instant visibility into **Total Revenue**, **Total Orders**, **Avg. Order-to-Delivery Time**, and **Avg. Customer Spend**.
* **Revenue by Occasion (Bar Chart):** Compares performance across major events like Anniversaries, Birthdays, and Diwali.
* **Ordering Trends (Line Chart):** Tracks revenue fluctuations by month and hour of the day.
* **City Leaderboard:** Ranks the top 10 cities by order volume to identify geographical growth opportunities.
* **Dynamic Filters:** Interactive Slicers for **Occasion** and **Date Timelines** that update all visuals in real-time.

### 6. Business Impact & Insights

* **Occasion Strategy:** Data revealed that certain categories, like "Sweets," significantly outperform others during Rakshabandhan, suggesting a need for increased inventory during this period.
* **Logistics Optimization:** Identifying cities with higher-than-average delivery times allows for better local courier partnerships.
* **Marketing Timing:** The discovery that ordering peaks occur late at night (8 PM - 11 PM) provides a strategic window for sending promotional app notifications.
